Privacy Policy

App: Miskia  |  Last updated: March 2026

1. No data collected

The Miskia app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All audio content is bundled locally inside the app. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no advertising of any kind.

2. No internet access

The app does not make any network requests. It works entirely offline after installation. No data is sent to any server, including Anthropic, Expo, or any third party.

3. Audio playback

The app plays music files that are stored inside the app package itself. It does not access your microphone, your music library, or any other media on your device.

4. Background audio

The app is allowed to continue playing audio when the screen is locked or when the user switches to another app. This is achieved through the iOS Background Audio capability. No data is collected during background playback.

5. Children's privacy

The app contains no objectionable content and does not target children. Since no personal information is collected at all, the app complies with COPPA and similar regulations by default.

6. Changes to this policy

If anything changes in a future update, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the new date.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be sent to michele.schiavo@boncafe.com.hk.
